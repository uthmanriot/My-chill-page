The 3 Highest-Rated Movies in 2025 across major critics' lists such as Roger Ebert, Metacritic, The New Yorker, and the National Society of Film Critics.

1. One Battle After Another

  • Genre: Crime / Drama / Thriller
  • Director: Paul Thomas Anderson
  • Considered a masterclass in directing and storytelling.
  • Features outstanding performances, led by Leonardo DiCaprio.
  • Balances action, emotion, politics, and dark humor without feeling bloated.
  • Won multiple Best Picture awards from major critics' organizations and topped several year-end lists.

2. Sinners

  • Genre: Horror / Thriller
  • Director: Ryan Coogler
  • Starring: Michael B. Jordan
  • Reinvents horror with strong themes about race, family, and identity.
  • Delivers genuine scares while telling an emotionally compelling story.
  • Praised for cinematography, performances, music, and originality.
  • One of the rare horror films to earn widespread critical acclaim and appear on many "Best of 2025" lists.

3. It Was Just an Accident

  • Genre: Drama
  • Director: Jafar Panahi
  • A deeply human and emotionally powerful film.
  • Explores justice, guilt, forgiveness, and life under oppression.
  • Critics praised its subtle storytelling and moral complexity rather than spectacle.
  • Frequently ranked among the year's very best by film critics
